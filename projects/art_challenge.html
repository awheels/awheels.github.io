ISZAF Art Challenge

I've been keen to experiment more with Processing, so when I heard about an art challenge being organized by the International Shenzhen Artist Forum (ISZAF), I was eager to participate. I do not identify as an expert coder nor an artist, but they are both areas that I was looking to grow in and further explore. The challenge consists of three phases.
